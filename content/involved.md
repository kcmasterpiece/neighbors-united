## Ways to Get Involved

### Community Recommendations

- Attend neighborhood meetings and invite one new resident each month.
- Share factual updates on social media and in local community groups.
- Write respectful letters to city officials and neighborhood associations.
- Volunteer to help with flyers, outreach, and event coordination.

### Quick Start

Start by signing up to receive updates. We will send meeting dates, action items, and opportunities to contribute.
